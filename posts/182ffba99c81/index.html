aoaotwitter-twitter关注限制策略

发表于2023-03-16|更新于2023-03-22|Aoao随笔生活

aoaotwitter-twitter关注限制策略

总览

Following accounts is an easy way to curate your Twitter feed and know what’s happening with the topics or people you’re interested in.
关注帐户是管理您的 Twitter 提要并了解您感兴趣的主题或人的动态的一种简单方法。
To prevent spam and manipulation of follow relationships and to ensure site reliability, there are technical limits regarding how many Twitter accounts you are able to follow.
为防止垃圾邮件和操纵关注关系并确保站点可靠性，对于您可以关注的 Twitter 帐户数量存在技术限制。
These limits are based on the rate at which you are following new accounts, as well as how many followers you have.
这些限制基于您关注新帐户的速度以及您拥有的关注者数量。
In addition, because follow relationships on Twitter aren’t mutual by nature, we’ve also put into place a few rules around follow behavior to promote authentic experiences.
此外，由于 Twitter 上的关注关系本质上不是相互的，我们还针对关注行为制定了一些规则，以促进真实体验。

What are the technical follow limits?
技术跟随限制是什么？

  • We don’t limit the number of accounts you can follow overall, but there are some limits as to the pace at which you can do so.
    我们不限制您总体上可以关注的帐户数量，但对您关注的速度有一些限制。
  • Every Twitter account is able to follow up to 400 accounts per day.
    每个 Twitter 帐户每天最多可以关注 400 个帐户。
  • In addition to the daily limits, there are follow ratios that go into effect once you’re following a certain number of accounts:
    除了每日限制之外，一旦您关注了一定数量的帐户，就会有关注比率生效：
    • Every Twitter account can follow up to 5,000 accounts. Once you reach that number, you may need to wait until your account has more followers before you can follow additional accounts.
      每个 Twitter 帐户最多可以关注 5,000 个帐户。达到该数字后，您可能需要等到您的帐户拥有更多关注者，然后才能关注其他帐户。
    • This number is different for each account and is automatically calculated based on your unique ratio of followers to following.
      这个数字对于每个帐户都是不同的，并且是根据您独特的关注者与关注者的独特比率自动计算的。

** Are there other rules that apply to follow behavior? 是否有其他适用于跟随行为的规则？ **

Yes. The Twitter Rules prohibit abusive following, and violation of these rules may result in the suspension of your account. Specifically, the rules prohibit:
是的。 Twitter 规则禁止滥用关注，违反这些规则可能会导致您的帐户被暂停。具体而言，规则禁止：

  • “follow churn” – following and then unfollowing large numbers of accounts in an effort to inflate one’s own follower count;
    “关注流失”——关注然后取消关注大量账户，以增加自己的关注者数量；
  • indiscriminate following – following and/or unfollowing a large number of unrelated accounts in a short time period, particularly by automated means;
    不分青红皂白地关注——在短时间内关注和/或取消关注大量不相关的账户，尤其是通过自动方式；
  • duplicating another account’s followers, particularly using automation; and
    复制另一个帐户的关注者，特别是使用自动化；和
  • using or promoting third-party services or apps that claim to add followers.
    使用或宣传声称可以增加关注者的第三方服务或应用程序。

Twitter ratio details 推特比例详情

You may encounter a message that states, “You are unable to follow more people at this time.” You’ll encounter this message for one of the following reasons:
您可能会遇到一条消息，指出“您此时无法关注更多人。”由于以下原因之一，您会遇到此消息：

  • You’ve reached the daily follow limit. You can follow more accounts after a day has passed.
    您已达到每日关注上限。一天过去后，您可以关注更多帐户。
  • You’ve followed too many accounts too quickly. Try again in an hour or so.
    您太快关注了太多帐户。大约一个小时后重试。
  • You’ve hit a follow ratio limit. You can try again once your account has more followers, or you can unfollow a few accounts to follow new ones.
    您已达到关注率限制。您可以在您的帐户拥有更多关注者后重试，或者您可以取消关注一些帐户以关注新帐户。
  • Your account is locked or limited. We may lock an account if appears to be compromised or if it is in violation of the Twitter Rules or Terms of Service, including due to aggressive follow behavior. Accounts in a locked state are limited in actions they can perform, including following. Read more about locked and limited accounts.
    您的帐户已被锁定或受限。如果帐户看起来受到威胁或违反 Twitter 规则或服务条款，包括由于激进的关注行为，我们可能会锁定帐户。处于锁定状态的帐户只能执行有限的操作，包括关注。阅读有关锁定和受限帐户的更多信息。

** Additional resources 额外资源 **

Learn more about our automation rules and best practices (intended for developers), and our following FAQs.
详细了解我们的自动化规则和最佳实践（面向开发人员）以及我们的以下常见问题解答。
